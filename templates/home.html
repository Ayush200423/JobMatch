How JobMatch Works

Sign in or create an account above. To use the auto application feature, download the JobMatch chrome extension linked in the navigation bar. Note: Your JobMatch account will be deactivated 30 days after the date of creation.

Upload your resume, and the Natural Language Processing (NLP) Machine Learning model will extract keywords.

Edit any inaccurate information and submit. Your information will be updated before applying to postings.

By using cosine similarity and word vectors to match your resume to jobs in the regularly updated job posting database, only the most relevant jobs will be picked out. Then, simply select the ones that you prefer and click submit.

Congratulations, you have just applied to job listings under one minute! Check your email for submitted job application(s).
